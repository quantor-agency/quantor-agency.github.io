Why We Exist
Most businesses deserve a website that actually
works for them — not one that sits there
looking pretty while leads walk out the door.

QUANTOR was built from a single, relentless frustration: watching talented businesses — restaurants, clinics, agencies, consultants — haemorrhage leads every night because their website did absolutely nothing after 6pm.

The technology to fix this already existed. The AI was already powerful enough. What was missing was someone willing to make it simple, fast, and affordable.

We are not a 50-person agency that charges $20,000 for a basic site and disappears for 6 weeks. We are a precision operation — lean, fast, and obsessively focused on one outcome: your business growing while you sleep.

Every project we take on is treated as if it were our own. Because the moment we stop caring about your results, we stop being QUANTOR.

How It Started

FROM ONE IDEA TO MANY RESULTS.

Every great thing starts somewhere small. QUANTOR started with a question that wouldn’t go away: why are so many brilliant businesses invisible online — and why isn’t anyone fixing it fast enough?

💡
The Spark — 2023
THE PROBLEM BECOMES CLEAR

After watching dozens of local businesses lose customers to competitors with smarter digital presence, the question became impossible to ignore: what if AI could level the playing field — for everyone?

⚙
Research — Early 2024
BUILDING THE SYSTEM

Months of testing AI tools, hosting solutions, chatbot platforms, and conversion frameworks. The goal: find the perfect stack that delivers enterprise results at small-business speed and price.

🚀
Launch — 2024
QUANTOR IS BORN

The first client went live in 3 days. The chatbot captured leads on day one. That result — a real business winning in real time — became the standard every project is held to.

🌟
Now — 2025
GROWING EVERY DAY

Six industries served. Multiple clients live. Every site built with the same obsessive standard: fast delivery, intelligent AI, and support that doesn’t vanish the moment the project closes.
