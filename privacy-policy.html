INFORMATION WE COLLECT

QUANTOR operates with a minimal data collection philosophy. We only collect information that is genuinely necessary to provide our services, process payments, communicate with you, and improve our offerings. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties under any circumstances.

Personal Information You Provide Directly:

  • Contact Information: Your name, email address, phone number, and WhatsApp number when you fill out our contact form, request a quote, or communicate with us directly.
  • Business Information: Your company name, industry, business description, and website URL when you submit a project brief or engage our services.
  • Project Details: Information about your project requirements, timeline expectations, budget range, and service preferences shared through our contact form or during consultations.
  • Payment Information: Billing details including name, email, and payment method information required to process transactions. Note: We do not store complete payment card details on our servers.
  • Communication Records: Records of our correspondence with you via email, WhatsApp, or other communication channels for service delivery and support purposes.

Information Collected Automatically:

  • Website Usage Data: Your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, pages visited, time spent on pages, and interaction patterns with our website elements.
  • Device Information: Basic information about the device you use to access our website, including device type, screen resolution, and operating system version.
  • Location Data: General geographic location based on your IP address (country, city, region). We do not collect precise GPS coordinates.

Important: Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages, which may collect basic access logs as part of their hosting infrastructure. GitHub's own privacy practices apply to their infrastructure. You can review GitHub's Privacy Statement at github.com/privacy.

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the information we collect for specific, legitimate purposes that are essential to providing our services and maintaining our business operations. Below is a comprehensive breakdown of each purpose:

Service Delivery & Project Execution:

  • To review your project requirements and provide accurate proposals, timelines, and cost estimates.
  • To communicate with you about your project status, deliverables, milestones, and any questions that arise during development.
  • To deliver the digital products and services you have purchased, including websites, AI chatbots, SEO services, and add-on features.

Payment Processing & Financial Records:

  • To process upfront and final payments through our approved payment methods (UPI, PayPal, Wise, or bank transfer).
  • To generate and send invoices, receipts, and payment confirmations.
  • To maintain accurate financial records for accounting, tax compliance, and audit purposes.
  • To handle refunds, chargebacks, or payment disputes in accordance with our Refund Policy.

Communication & Marketing:

  • To respond to your inquiries, support requests, and feedback in a timely manner.
  • To send project updates, delivery notifications, and service-related announcements.
  • To inform you about new services, features, or promotions (only if you have explicitly consented to receive marketing communications).
  • To follow up after project completion regarding your satisfaction and potential future needs.

Service Improvement & Quality Assurance:

  • To analyze website usage patterns and improve user experience, navigation, and content.
  • To identify common questions, pain points, or areas where our services could be enhanced.
  • To measure the effectiveness of our marketing efforts and optimize our outreach strategies.
  • To develop new services, features, or packages based on client needs and market trends.

Legal & Compliance:

  • To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes in India and internationally.
  • To enforce our Terms of Service and other contractual agreements.
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, and property, as well as those of our clients and partners.
  • To detect, prevent, and address fraud, unauthorized access, or other illegal activities.

Data Minimization Commitment: We do not collect more data than necessary for the purposes listed above. We do not profile users for targeted advertising, and we do not share your data with data brokers or advertising networks.

PAYMENT INFORMATION & PROCESSING

Payment security is a top priority at QUANTOR. We have implemented multiple safeguards to ensure your financial information is handled securely and in compliance with industry standards. Here is a detailed explanation of how we manage payment-related data:

Payment Methods We Accept:

  • UPI (Unified Payments Interface): For Indian clients, we accept payments via UPI through platforms like Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, and other UPI-enabled apps.
  • PayPal: For international clients, we accept PayPal payments which offer buyer protection and secure transaction processing.
  • Wise (formerly TransferWise): For international bank transfers with competitive exchange rates and transparent fees.
  • Bank Transfer: Direct bank transfers for larger transactions or clients who prefer this method.

Payment Flow & Data Handling:

  • 50% Upfront Payment: We collect 50% of the project cost before work begins. This confirms your commitment and allows us to allocate resources to your project.
  • 50% After Approval: The remaining 50% is collected only after you review and approve the final deliverables. This ensures you are satisfied before completing payment.
  • Payment Records: We retain records of all transactions including invoice numbers, payment amounts, dates, and payment methods for accounting and legal purposes.

What We Do NOT Store:

  • Complete credit card numbers, CVV codes, or card expiration dates.
  • Bank account login credentials or passwords.
  • UPI PIN numbers or authentication codes.
  • PayPal or Wise account passwords.

Payment Security Measures:

  • All payment transactions are processed through PCI-DSS compliant payment processors (PayPal, Wise) or secure banking channels (UPI).
  • We never request sensitive payment information via unsecured channels such as plain text email or unencrypted messages.
  • Payment confirmations and invoices are sent via email with appropriate security measures.
  • We maintain separate records for financial transactions that are not linked to your marketing or service usage data.

AI CHATBOT SERVICES

QUANTOR provides AI-powered chatbot services through our partnership with Chatling AI. This section explains how data is handled specifically for clients who use our chatbot services, whether as a standalone product or integrated into a website package.

About Our Chatbot Provider:

  • We use Chatling AI as our primary chatbot platform, which offers access to 32+ AI language models.
  • Chatling AI is a third-party service provider with its own privacy policy and data handling practices.
  • When you purchase a chatbot from QUANTOR, both our privacy policy and Chatling AI's terms apply to the service.
  • You can review Chatling AI's privacy documentation at chatling.ai.

Data Collection by Chatbots:

  • Visitor Interactions: Messages typed by visitors to your website when they interact with the chatbot, including questions, responses, and any contact information they share.
  • Lead Information: Email addresses, phone numbers, or other contact details visitors provide through the chatbot's lead capture forms.
  • Conversation History: Records of chat sessions for training purposes, quality improvement, and support analytics.
  • Training Data: Business information you provide to train the chatbot, including FAQs, product details, service descriptions, and company knowledge base.

How Chatbot Data is Used:

  • To provide instant responses to your website visitors' questions based on your business data.
  • To capture and forward leads to you via email or WhatsApp notifications.
  • To improve chatbot response accuracy through machine learning and conversation analysis.
  • To generate analytics reports about visitor engagement, common questions, and lead conversion rates.

Your Responsibilities as a Chatbot Client:

  • Disclosure: You must inform your website visitors that they are interacting with an AI chatbot and that their conversations may be recorded or analyzed.
  • Training Data Accuracy: You are responsible for ensuring that the information you provide to train the chatbot is accurate, up-to-date, and does not contain misleading or harmful content.
  • Privacy Compliance: You are responsible for ensuring your chatbot implementation complies with privacy laws applicable to your business and visitor locations.
  • Lead Handling: Any personal information captured through the chatbot becomes your responsibility to handle according to applicable privacy laws.

Chatbot Data Retention:

  • Conversation logs and analytics are retained by Chatling AI according to their retention policy.
  • You can request deletion of specific conversation data by contacting us.
  • Training data can be updated or removed at your request during your subscription period.
  • Upon termination of chatbot services, you can request complete removal of your business data from the chatbot system.

Important for Clients: While QUANTOR sets up and configures your chatbot, the ongoing data collection by the chatbot is primarily governed by Chatling AI's policies. We recommend reviewing their terms and implementing appropriate privacy notices on your website.

DATA STORAGE & SECURITY

We take comprehensive measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This section outlines our data storage practices and security protocols:

Where Your Data is Stored:

  • Website Hosting: Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages (Microsoft Azure infrastructure), which provides enterprise-grade security and reliability.
  • Email Communications: Email correspondence is stored in secure email accounts with two-factor authentication enabled.
  • WhatsApp Communications: Business communications via WhatsApp are stored on encrypted devices and backed up securely.
  • Payment Records: Financial records are stored in secure accounting software with encrypted backups.
  • Project Files: Client project files, deliverables, and documentation are stored in secure cloud storage with access controls.
  • Chatbot Data: As mentioned, chatbot-related data is stored on Chatling AI's secure servers.

Security Measures We Implement:

  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel with a legitimate business need have access to client data. Access is logged and regularly audited.
  • Authentication: All accounts and systems used for client data are protected by strong passwords and two-factor authentication.
  • Encryption: Sensitive communications and files are encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL and at rest where applicable.
  • Secure Deletion: When data retention periods expire, we ensure secure deletion using industry-standard methods.
  • Regular Updates: All software, plugins, and systems are kept up-to-date with the latest security patches.
  • Backup Procedures: Regular encrypted backups ensure data can be recovered in case of system failures or security incidents.

Data Breach Response:

In the unlikely event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we commit to:

  • Investigating the breach immediately upon discovery to determine its scope and impact.
  • Containing the breach and implementing measures to prevent further unauthorized access.
  • Notifying affected individuals within 72 hours if the breach poses a risk to their rights and freedoms.
  • Providing clear information about what happened, what data was affected, and what steps we are taking.
  • Offering guidance on protective measures you can take, such as changing passwords or monitoring accounts.
  • Reporting the breach to relevant authorities as required by applicable laws.

YOUR RIGHTS & CHOICES

You have specific rights regarding your personal information. We are committed to facilitating these rights and responding to your requests promptly. Here is a comprehensive overview of your rights:

Right to Access:

  • You have the right to request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • We will provide this information in a commonly used electronic format within 30 days of a verified request.
  • There is no charge for this service unless the request is manifestly unfounded or excessive.

Right to Correction:

  • You have the right to request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information.
  • We will update your information promptly upon verification of the correct details.
  • We may require you to provide evidence of the correct information in some cases.

Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten):

  • You have the right to request deletion of your personal information in certain circumstances.
  • This right applies when: the data is no longer necessary; you withdraw consent; the data was unlawfully processed; or erasure is required by law.
  • We may retain certain information where there is a legal obligation (e.g., financial records for tax compliance) or legitimate business interest.
  • When we erase your data, we will also instruct relevant third parties to do the same where practicable.

Right to Restrict Processing:

  • You have the right to request that we restrict processing of your personal information in certain circumstances.
  • During restriction, we may store your data but not process it further without your consent.

Right to Data Portability:

  • You have the right to receive your personal information in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.
  • You also have the right to transmit this data to another service provider where technically feasible.

Right to Object:

  • You have the right to object to processing of your personal information for direct marketing purposes.
  • We will stop processing your data for marketing immediately upon receiving such a request.
  • You may also object to processing based on legitimate interests where your rights override our interests.

Right to Withdraw Consent:

  • Where we rely on consent as the legal basis for processing, you have the right to withdraw that consent at any time.
  • Withdrawal of consent does not affect the lawfulness of processing that occurred before withdrawal.
  • You can withdraw consent by contacting us through the methods listed below.

Right to Lodge a Complaint:

  • If you believe your data protection rights have been violated, you have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.
  • In India, you can contact the Data Protection Authority of India or relevant authorities under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act.
  • For EU residents, you can contact the data protection authority in your member state of residence.
