Common questions

Who owns the chatbot and website after delivery?
You own everything. We purchase your Chatling account and hosting plan on your behalf, set everything up, and hand over every login and credential to you. We don't hold any of your data except the business information needed to build your project. Your accounts, your passwords, your business.
What does "retraining" mean?
Retraining means updating your chatbot's knowledge — adding new services, changing prices, updating FAQs, adding new responses. Monthly plan users get 1 session included per month. Annual plan users get 2 sessions per year. Extra sessions are $20 (monthly) or $25 (annual).
What is GitHub Pages hosting on the Starter plan?
GitHub Pages is a free, reliable hosting service by GitHub (owned by Microsoft). Your site lives at a free web address. It's fast, secure, and costs nothing. If you want a proper custom domain (like yoursalon.com) and traditional hosting, that's an optional upgrade — we purchase it for you and hand over all credentials.
What does "3 months free support" mean on the Pro plan?
If anything on your website breaks or has an issue caused by our work, we fix it for free within 3 months of delivery. After 3 months, fixes are $18 per session.
Do you do maintenance on the Starter plan?
The Starter plan is a one-time delivery. We cover minor fixes for 30 days after delivery. After that, any changes or fixes are billed per session. It's built to be simple enough that most clients never need changes.
How do I pay?
India: UPI, Razorpay, debit/credit card. International: PayPal or Wise. 50% upfront before work begins, 50% on delivery.
What if I already have a website and just need the chatbot?
We build and train your chatbot, then give you the embed code to paste into your existing website. Once it's live and working, we hand over your Chatling account and credentials. Simple.
