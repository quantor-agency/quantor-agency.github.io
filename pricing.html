Pricing FAQ

What is included in "done for you"?
Everything. We handle design, development, domain setup, hosting configuration, chatbot training, and deployment. You provide the business information, we deliver a complete, working solution. No technical work required on your end.
Do I need to pay for Tidio separately?
No. The Tidio Lyro AI subscription is included in your plan and managed by us. You don't need to create a separate account or worry about billing. We handle everything for you.
What happens when my annual plan renews?
We'll contact you 30 days before renewal to confirm continuation. Your renewal fee covers the cost of hosting, domain, and chatbot subscription — no extra hidden charges or service fees added. Your site and chatbot continue running without interruption.
Can I upgrade from Landing to Pro later?
Yes. You can upgrade anytime by paying the difference between plans. We'll expand your landing page into a full website while preserving your existing design and chatbot configuration.
Is the domain and hosting mine to keep?
The domain is registered in your name and belongs to you. Hosting is provided for the duration of your plan. If you ever want to move to another provider, we'll help transfer everything to you.
How do I pay?
We accept multiple payment methods for your convenience. For Indian customers: Razorpay, UPI, and Debit/Credit cards. For international customers: PayPal and Wise. Payment is collected after we discuss your requirements and before work begins. 50% upfront, 50% on delivery for site plans.
