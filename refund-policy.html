1. Overview

QUANTOR delivers custom digital services including web development, AI chatbot integration, and digital solutions tailored to your business needs. Due to the bespoke nature of our work, refunds are subject to the conditions outlined below. We are committed to transparency and fairness in all our dealings.

2. Before Work Begins

Eligible for Refund
If you cancel before work has started on your project, you are eligible for a full refund within 48 hours of payment. To qualify, you must submit your cancellation request in writing before our team begins any design, development, or planning activities.

Full refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to the original payment method.

3. After Work Begins

Not Eligible for Refund
Once work has started on your project — including but not limited to design mockups, development, coding, or chatbot training and configuration — no refund will be issued. This policy exists because time, tools, resources, and skilled labor have already been committed to your project.

We document all project milestones and will keep you informed at each stage of development.

4. Revision Policy

If the final deliverable does not match the agreed brief or project specifications, we will revise it at no additional charge. Our goal is your complete satisfaction with the final product. However, we do not issue refunds as a substitute for revisions.

All revision requests must be submitted within 14 days of project delivery.

5. Chatbot Subscription Plans

Monthly Plan

You may cancel anytime. No refund is provided for the current billing month, but cancellation prevents future charges.

Annual Plan

No refund is available after the initial setup is complete. You retain access to the service until the end of your billing period.

6. Exceptional Circumstances

Our Commitment
In rare cases where QUANTOR fails to deliver the agreed-upon services entirely, a full refund will be issued. These exceptional circumstances are handled on a case-by-case basis with full transparency. We take our commitments seriously and will work with you to find a fair resolution.

7. How to Request

To request a cancellation or refund, please email us at quantorleads@gmail.com with the following information:

  • Your full name
  • Order or project reference number
  • Date of purchase
  • Reason for cancellation or refund request

We respond to all requests within 2 business days.
